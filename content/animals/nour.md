Nour (meaning "light" in Arabic) is a young orange tabby kitten rescued from a construction site in January 2025. Workers had been feeding her informally for weeks before the site was due to be cleared.

She arrived underweight but in good spirits — curious and playful from day one.

## Medical Status

Nour has received her first round of kitten vaccinations. She will need boosters over the next two months. She is healthy and growing fast.

## Personality

Nour is full of energy and mischief. She loves to explore, climb, and play with feather toys. She would do best in a home with another young cat or a patient owner who can give her plenty of stimulation.

**Nour is ready for adoption or fostering.**
