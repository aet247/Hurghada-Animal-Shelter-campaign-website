Today is a day I've been working toward for years.

My name is Ahmed Fathi. I'm a lawyer based here in Hurghada, Egypt. For as long as I can remember, I've watched stray dogs, cats, and donkeys struggle to survive on our streets — in the summer heat, through winters, near traffic, without reliable food or water or care.

I decided to do something about it.

## What We're Building

We have secured a plot of land on the outskirts of Hurghada. The plan is to build a structured, staffed animal shelter that can:

- Rescue stray and abandoned animals from the streets
- Provide food, water, and medical care
- Vaccinate and neuter animals to reduce the stray population humanely
- Rehome animals with loving families — locally and internationally

## Why GoGetFunding?

We chose GoGetFunding because it allows us to collect donations transparently and from donors worldwide. Every single contribution is tracked here on this website — you can see exactly how your money is being spent.

## What We Need

To get the shelter operational, we need funds for:

1. **Construction** — fencing, kennels, shelter structures
2. **Medical supplies** — vaccines, medications, surgical equipment
3. **Food** — ongoing feeding for 20-30 animals initially
4. **Staff** — a part-time vet and caretaker

## Our First Three Animals

We've already taken in three animals while we finalize the shelter construction — Luna, Max, and Nour. You can read their stories on our [Animals page](/animals).

## How to Help Right Now

1. **Donate** — any amount helps, even $10 covers a week of food for one animal
2. **Share** — post our campaign link on social media
3. **Volunteer** — if you're in Hurghada, we need hands

Thank you for being here. This is just the beginning.

— Ahmed
